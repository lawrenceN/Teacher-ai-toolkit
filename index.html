About Teacher AI Toolkit

The Teacher AI Toolkit is a curated collection of resources, guides, and tools designed to help educators understand and effectively use AI technologies in the classroom. Whether you're just starting with AI or looking to enhance your existing practice, we provide the resources you need to succeed.
